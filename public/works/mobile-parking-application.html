Mobile Parking Application

A mobile application designed to modernize parking management and municipal services for users in the southern region of Malaysia.

The problem

Users relied on physical parking coupons and paper-based licenses, which were easy to lose, inconvenient to manage, and difficult to renew. Information about parking rules, municipal updates, and local issues was fragmented and often outdated.

The solution

A centralized mobile application that allows users to purchase parking coupons digitally, manage premise licenses, and access official announcements in one place. The app reduces dependency on physical tickets while improving accessibility and transparency.

Key features

  • • Digital parking coupon purchase and management
  • • Premise license application and renewal
  • • Transaction history and digital records
  • • News, bulletins, and municipal announcements
  • • User-friendly mobile interface for daily use

Impact

The application simplifies daily parking activities, reduces reliance on physical documents, and provides a reliable communication channel between local authorities and the public. Users gain convenience, while municipalities benefit from better data visibility and operational efficiency.
