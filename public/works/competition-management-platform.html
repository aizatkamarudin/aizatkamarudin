Competition Management Platform

A centralized application designed to manage competitive events from participant registration through scheduling, execution, and result tracking, all within a single system.

The problem

Event organizers relied on multiple disconnected tools and manual coordination to manage registrations, schedules, and communications. This created delays, limited visibility into event progress, and increased the risk of errors during live competitions.

The solution

A unified competition management platform that centralizes registrations, event schedules, match timing, and internal communication. The system simplifies coordination and provides real-time access to event data for organizers and participants.

Key features

  • • Participant registration and management
  • • Event and match scheduling
  • • Real-time match timing and updates
  • • Internal chat and communication tools
  • • Leaderboard and performance tracking

Role & responsibility

Led the mobile application development, coordinated technical implementation, and ensured the platform met performance, usability, and scalability requirements throughout the project lifecycle.

Impact

The platform improved operational efficiency, reduced manual coordination, and provided clear visibility into event progress and results. It enabled organizers to manage competitions more reliably while enhancing the participant experience.
