Smart Reporting Application

A mobile-first reporting application designed for electrical contractors to replace manual reporting processes with a structured, digital workflow.

The problem

Electrical contractors relied heavily on manual documentation and fragmented reporting methods. Reports were time-consuming to prepare, prone to human error, and difficult to track or analyze across multiple projects and teams.

The solution

A smart reporting application that digitalizes data collection, analysis, and report generation. The system centralizes reporting workflows, allowing users to generate accurate reports efficiently without relying on manual paperwork.

Key features

  • • Digital report creation and management
  • • Automated data collection and validation
  • • Structured report templates for consistency
  • • Centralized report history and tracking
  • • Mobile-friendly interface for field use

Impact

The application reduced manual workload, improved reporting accuracy, and increased productivity across teams. Contractors were able to focus more on analysis and decision-making rather than administrative tasks.
